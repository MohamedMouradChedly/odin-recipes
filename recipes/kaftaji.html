Kaftaji

Kaftaji is a renowned and beloved traditional Tunisian dish celebrated for its rich flavor, derived from a diverse array of ingredients, and its satisfying heartiness, guaranteed to satiate your appetite.

Ingredients:

  • 300 grams of Potato
  • 300 grams of Pumpkin
  • 250 grams of Sweet Pepper
  • 250 grams of Tender Meat
  • 200 grams of Tomato
  • 100 grams of Hot Pepper
  • 50 grams of Coriander
  • 4 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Half a tablespoon of Ornament Pepper
  • Half a tablespoon of Spice Blend
  • Half a tablespoon of Mint Leaves
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 sour Lemon
  • Frying Oil

Steps:

  1. Crush the garlic and peel it with a little salt.
  2. Grind the meat and season it with ornament pepper and black pepper Then it is diced and turned into meatballs the size of a walnut.
  3. Peel the potatoes and cut them into slices.
  4. Clean a portion of the pumpkin and cut it into thin slices.
  5. Clean the sweet pepper, remove its seeds, and veins.
  6. Wash the tomatoes, cut them in half, and then squeeze them by hand to remove the moisture and seeds.
  7. Heat frying oil in a deep pan and initially fry the meatballs then leave them aside.
  8. Remove the tops and the seeds of the hot peppers, season the insides with a bit of spice and salt, then fry them in oil and set them aside.
  9. Fry the remaining vegetables, cut them into small pieces, fry the eggs one by one, and lift them with a spatula to avoid altering their shape."
